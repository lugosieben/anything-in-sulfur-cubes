## Special Behaviors introduced in Anything in Sulfur Cube
Generated for Minecraft 26.2

Next to nearly any block having its own properties in a Sulfur Cube, this pack also introduces custom behavior for some blocks!

### Cactus

Touching a Cactus Sulfur Cube will damage you like a normal cactus.

### Creaking Heart

Creaking Heart Sulfur Cubes will play eerie sounds without actually spawning a creaking.

### Potent Sulfur

Sulfur Cubes with Potent Sulfur will give the nausea effect while in contact.
